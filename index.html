Serious Finch

Serious Finch is a hip-hop artist and songwriter from Brooklyn, New York. Known for gritty street-style rap and collaborations with artists like Nature Finch and Khyian Wayne, he has appeared on songs such as “Work Week,” “Where You From,” and “Finch Boys Freestyle.”
